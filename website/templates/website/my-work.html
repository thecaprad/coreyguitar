“Shot in the Dark” — Tragic Gadget

X

Guitar parts

This song was written by LA friends Kim Tilman and Chris Lorentz who go by the collective name Tragic Gadget. As they wrote this song, I dropped by their recording space in Hollywood and was asked to record layer upon layer of different ideas.

I love what they ultimately selected, cut up and processed. The guitar parts sound to me like a child between a Katy Perry and Jackson Five song — good things in my book.

The animation was done by a group of San Francisco-based creators, Abraham Dieckman, Timothy Hahn and Pamela Goodman. (Chinese Takeout Animation)
